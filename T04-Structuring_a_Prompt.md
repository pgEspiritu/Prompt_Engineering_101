### 1️⃣ Clarity

A well-defined prompt includes relevant context to reduce ambiguity.

---

### 2️⃣ Specificity

# 🧩 Elements of a Prompt

## 🧭 1. Instructions

At least one element of a strong prompt should include clear instructions.

Clear instructions reduce ambiguity.

### Example

❌ Vague:
> "What is the name of the monarch?"

✔️ Clear:
> "What is the name of the current monarch in Denmark?"

This narrows the response domain.

> 📷 **Image:** DALLE-3

---

## 🎭 2. Persona

Adopting a persona can help shape output style, relevance, and usefulness.

### Example

❌ Broad:
> "Write me a short story"

✔️ Persona-based:
> "You are J.K. Rowling. Write me a short story about magic."

This encourages ChatGPT to adopt a specific writing style, improving output quality.

---

## 📦 3. Output Format

We can explicitly define the format of the response:

- Summary
- Detailed explanation
- Checklist
- Steps
- Bullet points

### Example

> "I’m applying for a job as a data scientist. Create a checklist for how I can best prepare."

By specifying format, ChatGPT tailors its output structure accordingly.

> 📷 **Image:** DALLE-3

---

## 🧭 4. Context

Providing background information helps ChatGPT generate more accurate insights.

### Example

> "Here is the transcript from *How to Breathe Correctly for Optimal Health, Mood, Learning & Performance* by Huberman Lab. What does it say about physiological sighs?"

ChatGPT can quickly extract relevant insights from large amounts of text.

---

## ✍️ 5. Examples

Providing examples helps ChatGPT learn tone, structure, and style.

### Example Prompt

> "Here’s an example of a typical email response in my writing style: {insert example email}.  
Use my writing style demonstrated in the example email to reply to the following message: {insert email you want to respond to}."

This is one of the most powerful prompting techniques for personalizing output.
