# 🧩 A Helping Hand for Problem-Solving

Starting a difficult problem alone can often feel overwhelming.

You may find yourself stuck, unsure where to begin.

Fortunately, a technique called **Chain-of-Thought Prompting** can transform ChatGPT into a powerful problem-solving assistant.

---

## 🔗 What Is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-thought prompting encourages ChatGPT to break a complex problem into smaller, manageable steps.

Rather than jumping straight to the answer, the model walks through the reasoning process.

---

## 🛠️ Two Approaches

### 1️⃣ Zero-Shot Chain-of-Thought

Provide no examples and ask ChatGPT to generate a step-by-step solution.

Example:

> "Break this problem down step by step."

---

### 2️⃣ One-Shot / Few-Shot Chain-of-Thought

Provide one or more examples of a reasoning framework.

ChatGPT can then apply the same framework to solve new problems.

# ⚠️ Limitations

Like any tool, ChatGPT has limitations.

Understanding these limitations helps you become a more effective user.

---

## ⚖️ Biases

The model may reflect stereotypes or misinformation present in its training data.

---

## 🌀 Hallucinations

Sometimes the model confidently presents incorrect information as though it were factual.

---

## 📚 Overfitting to Training Data

The model is only as good as the information it has been trained on.

It may struggle with:

- New developments
- Specialized knowledge
- Missing context

---

## 👀 Developing a Critical Eye

Recognizing these shortcomings will help you:

- Verify information
- Ask better follow-up questions
- Use ChatGPT more effectively
